Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Thank you for using 图识 ("the App"). We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Overview

图识 is designed with a privacy-first approach. The App primarily operates offline and does not collect personal data for tracking or advertising purposes.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to developer-controlled servers.

The App does not require account registration and does not build user profiles.

Data generated by the App (such as OCR results or imported dictionary files) is stored locally on your device unless you enable iCloud synchronization.

3. Photo Library Permission

The App may request access to your photo library for the following user-initiated features:

  • Selecting images for OCR word recognition
  • Choosing a profile avatar image
  • Adding images within user-created groups for collection and record purposes

For all of the above scenarios:

  • Image processing occurs locally on your device.
  • No photos are uploaded to developer-controlled servers.
  • The App does not scan your photo library in the background.
  • Images remain stored only on your device and/or your personal iCloud (if enabled).
  • You can revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

4. iCloud Synchronization

If you choose to enable iCloud, certain app data may be synchronized using Apple's iCloud services.

  • Data is transmitted directly between your device and Apple.
  • The developer does not have access to your iCloud content.
  • iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy policies and terms.

You can disable iCloud at any time in your device settings.

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and yearly) that unlock the full built-in dictionary (approximately 360,000 entries).

  • Payments are processed by Apple via your App Store account.
  • The developer does not receive or store your payment information.
  • Subscription status is validated using Apple StoreKit.

Users can continue to import and use their own MDX dictionaries without subscribing.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because most data is stored locally on your device, you may delete app data at any time by:

  • Removing imported files within the App
  • Deleting the App from your device
  • Disabling iCloud synchronization (if enabled)

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
